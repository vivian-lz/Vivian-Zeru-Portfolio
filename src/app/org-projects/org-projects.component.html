Wearable Sensor for Health Monitoring

I am in the Du Group Research Lab, run by my PI, Dr. Du. The first project I worked on was to learn how to solder using SMD reflow and a hot air station in order to assemble a PCB with microscopic component sizes. This wearable was created for human health monitoring in order to gain real-life physiological insights. It utilizes EEG and EMG to measure brain activity, ECG to measure cardiovascular data, and NIRS to measure data realted to blood and tissue oxygenation. I am currently working on learning circuit design to hopefully design sensors in the future!
